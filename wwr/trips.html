What you need to bring for each trip
Trip Need to bring
1 - 2 Hour Adventure sunscreen, clothes you can get wet in, waterproof bag for any items you want to bring and not get wet, sandals with straps or water shoes
3 - 4 Hour Adventure sunscreen, clothes you can get wet in, waterproof bag for any items you want to bring and not get wet, sandals with straps or water shoes, waterbottles with a sturdy handle, lunch in a plastic ziplock bag
Half Day Adventure sunscreen, clothes you can get wet in, waterproof bag for any items you want to bring and not get wet, sandals with straps or water shoes, waterbottles with a sturdy handle, lunch and dinner(if you want) in a plastic ziplock bag
Overnight Adventure sunscreen, clothes you can get wet in, waterproof bag for any items you want to bring and not get wet, sandals with straps or water shoes, waterbottles with a sturdy handle; lunch, dinner, and snacks(if you want) in a plastic ziplock bag, overnight camping gear such as a tent or hammock, sleaping bag, sleeping pad, etc. that you will to sleep along with toiletries and a change of clothes in a duffle bag with your name on it
2 Night Adventure sunscreen, clothes you can get wet in, waterproof bag for any items you want to bring and not get wet, sandals with straps or water shoes, waterbottles with a sturdy handle, lunch and dinner(if you want) in a plastic ziplock bag, overnight camping gear such as a tent or hammock, sleaping bag, sleeping pad, etc. that you will to sleep along with toiletries and a change of clothes in a duffle bag with your name on it
